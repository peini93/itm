ABOUT ITM

關於ITM

Established January 2019, International Trust Machines Co., Ltd. provides a blockchain scaling solution and IC enabler. Confronting the exponential rise of both IoT devices and blockchain ledger technology, ITM works in the convergence of the blockchain with the Internet of Things. With its patented Merkle Tree technology and ledger, ITM has broken the blockchain bandwidth limit, enabled rapid IC search software, allows on-chain IoT equipment data networking and innovated Machine to Machine account auditing capabilities. ITM can now clear millions of data with a single transaction on the blockchain, achieving immutability for trustworthy and legitimate information.

CORE
TECHNOLOGY

核心技術

ITM breaks through blockchain's three biggest problems

ITM Blockchain Scaling Solution

Uploads the fingerprint of a large amount of transaction data onto the public blockchain, verifying data accuracy with an open source program. A single successful transaction on the public chain can clear a chain of millions of data while ensuring that the data has not been tampered with.

ITM Blockchain IC Enabler

Using ITM's security protocol, a cryptographic security algorithm is placed in IC architecture for wider expansion, higher security, privacy protection and low-cost IoT uplink demand.

SCENARIO

應用場景

Blockchain Battery Management System

Using ITM's scaling solution and blockchain IC technology, we will establish a health history for large-scale vehicle batteries, fair standards for multinational trading prices and transaction prices for used and third-hand batteries.

Blockchain Smart Meter

Using ITM's scaling solution and blockchain IC technology, current smart meter data is uploaded to the blockchain to ensure its safety and integrity. Fujitsu's application of blockchain technology in Japan improved power control and increased the demand dispatch success rate by 40%.

Blockchain Smart Transportation

Using ITM's scaling solution and blockchain IC technology, driving information can be linked and used to create public trust. This system can be used for fleet management, logistics control, supply-chain finance and transportation governance.
